ABOUT
At present, the human race has become increasingly dependent on technology to the point where we are often at its mercy. From daily tasks like waking up needing our phones, to simply passing the time with social media, as well as using our gadgets for school, work, and entertainment. This massive dependence creates a loop where we are just purely dependent on technology itself. Technology was created to serve humans, but the ongoing trend seems to show that humans are the slaves, and technology is the master, giving it not just our time, but also our efforts and attention.
In addition, this dependence on technology can also affect us in our relationships. Some people spend hours on their phone instead of actually talking to somebody face-to-face, while some struggle being offline even for just a short moment. So in a way, this tool that was made to make our lives easier may also make them worse in a lot of ways. Although technology has a lot of benefits, sometimes, we need to actually think about the drawbacks. Otherwise, we risk being reliant on it like slaves, rather than using it for something better.
